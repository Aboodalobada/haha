What are the school study hours?

School hours start from Monday to Thursday from 7:15 in the morning until 2:27 in the afternoon.

How many grades does the school have?

The school includes boys and girls in the girls’ branch from Kindergarten 1 to grade 4, and then girls only until grade 12, while the boys’ branch includes students from grades 5 to 12.

Can you mention the range of tuition fees charged by the school?

The tuition fees at Al Shoula School for Boys in Sharjah range between AED 6,000 and AED 11,500, while the tuition fees at Al Shoula Private School for Girls Sharjah in Al Falah range between AED 5,600 and AED 15,800.

How to communicate with parents?

Al Shula Private School Sharjah, with its two branches, is keen on the importance of communication between parents and the teaching and administrative staff to ensure effective education for students through updating the school’s website and launching an advanced program as a means of communication alongside the Parents’ Council and the school’s WhatsApp groups, among others.
